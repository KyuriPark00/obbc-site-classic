Who We Are

Our Beliefs in Action: Worship, and Mission

A Church Where
Truth and Love Meet

Open Bible Baptist Church has been serving Aylmer, Ontario and the surrounding communities for over 40 years.

We are a church where the love of God’s Word and the love of people meet together.

We invite you to join us, and to see the difference that the Word of God makes in your life. Whether your newly searching to find something more to life, to understand more of who God is, or your desiring to grow in your faith in Christ through the living truths of the Bible, our doors are open to you.
